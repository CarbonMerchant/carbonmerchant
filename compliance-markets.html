Offering 01 — Canadian Markets

Canadian Carbon and Clean Fuel Compliance Markets

Origination, structuring, and execution across Alberta TIER, BC OBPS, the federal Clean Fuel Regulations, and BC LCFS — for the desks that create credits and the desks that need them.

What We Do

Scope of the mandate.

  • Credit marketing & placement — we take your credits to named counterparties, negotiate structure, and close — with our fee tracking your realized price.
  • Compliance procurement strategy — a plan set against your obligation curve and risk appetite, not deadline-driven buying.
  • Bilateral negotiation & execution — term sheets to signed agreements, with pricing logic grounded in live market levels.
  • Registry & settlement support — through delivery, so the trade that was negotiated is the trade that settles.
  • Portfolio & multi-market planning — banked credits, forward exposure, and positions across TIER, OBPS, CFR, and LCFS managed as one book.
Who It's For

Credit creators and obligated parties.

Project developers and producers with compliance credits at, approaching, or past issuance; regulated emitters, primary suppliers, and obligated parties managing recurring exposure. We engage post-FID, at validation, at issuance, and post-issuance.

How an Engagement Runs

Four steps. No mystery.

Working session

Thirty confidential minutes. You bring the position; you leave with a market read either way.

Mandate & structure

Scope, economics, and the hat we're wearing — agency or advisory — agreed in writing before any work begins.

Market execution

Origination, negotiation, and pricing in our name. Your identity stays out of the market.

Confirmation & settlement

You contract directly with your chosen counterparty; we support through registry and settlement.

Discretion Is the Product

Through price discovery and negotiation, the market sees Carbon Merchant — not you. Your name is disclosed only at confirmation, to the one counterparty you've chosen to contract with. Everyone you didn't trade with never learns you were in the market.

We engage where commercial outcomes are decided — post-FID, at validation, at issuance, and post-issuance — and we execute quietly. No client is named, referenced, or implied in our marketing, our conversations, or our content. Ever.
