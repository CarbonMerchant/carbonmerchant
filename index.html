How We Work

Agency. Advisory.

We work two ways: agency or advisory. You'll know which one before we start.

Agency

Your outsourced commercial arm

We represent your credits or your procurement mandate in market — origination, negotiation, and execution under a clear marketing agreement. Through discovery and negotiation, the market deals with Carbon Merchant; your name enters only at confirmation, with the counterparty you contract with.

Our fee tracks your realized price.
Advisory

Your commercial bench

Retained or project-based counsel on structure, pricing logic, and market timing — for teams that need a desk's judgment without building one.

Judgment from live flow, not reports.

Discretion Is the Product

Through price discovery and negotiation, the market sees Carbon Merchant — not you. Your name is disclosed only at confirmation, to the one counterparty you've chosen to contract with. Everyone you didn't trade with never learns you were in the market.

We engage where commercial outcomes are decided — post-FID, at validation, at issuance, and post-issuance — and we execute quietly. No client is named, referenced, or implied in our marketing, our conversations, or our content. Ever.
